Empowering Women Cooks

Many gifted Moroccan women have recipes and skills passed down through generations, but lack the resources to open a restaurant or reach a wider audience. Your support helps them access training, equipment, and opportunities to share their cuisine with the world.

Supporting Cooperatives

Cooperatives are the heart of many Moroccan communities. They bring women together to produce traditional foods, crafts, and more. Donations help these cooperatives grow, sustain families, and keep Moroccan traditions alive.
